## 4. Overall Trend Analysis
- **Alpha Generation**: Seohak-100 tends to follow the Nasdaq-100 closely but with higher beta. During strong bull markets, it often outperforms NDX because retail investors concentrate on the year's top-performing winners.
- **Risk Profile**: In downturns (like 2022), the index may see sharper pullbacks than S&P 500 due to its tech-heavy and growth-oriented nature.
- **Conclusion**: The Seohak-100 is an 'Aggressive Tech' index that effectively captures the collective wisdom (and momentum) of individual Korean investors.
